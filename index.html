About me

Welcome! I'm Brandon Bautista, a frontend focused, web and app developer. I'm dedicated to the art of bringing a project to life, from crafting clean code to designing visually aesthetic projects, all while ensuring that usability remains a top priority.

After completing my business degree, I ventured into digital marketing as a freelancer. Technical Projects

NexusMD

A native application designed to help medical practices manage patient information, appointments, and medical records. It features secure login for staff and patients, appointment scheduling and reminders, electronic medical records, plus insurance and billing information. Technologies used include React.js, C#, and MongoDB.


GymAlpha

A web and mobile application that helps weightlifters track their progress and reach their fitness goals. It features workout tracking, progress monitoring, and personalized workout plans. Users can log the weight and reps of each exercise and track their progress over time. It uses React.js, Node.js and MongoDB.


Contempo Design

A web-based application that serves as my personal system of guidelines, components, and tools for designing intuitive, modern, and responsive user interfaces. It is heavily inspired by some of the best practices of user interface design from industry leaders such as Apple Developer, Base Web, and Material UI. Technologies used include React.js, Node.js, and Wordpress.
